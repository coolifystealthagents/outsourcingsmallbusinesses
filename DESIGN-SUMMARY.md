# Design summary

- Design concept: Small business operator
- Layout personality: local service business
- Typography: Aptos Display headings with Aptos body text
- Color palette: primary #164e63, secondary #67e8f9, accent #f97316, background #ecfeff, text #083344, muted #64748b
- Shape language: Slight rounding
- Visual motif: checklists and owner notes
- Unique layout choices: first sections use Owner playbook, then a custom service index, process ribbon, question block, and resource strip with varied widths and alignments.
- Difference from previous sites: this redesign changes the hero composition, section ordering, image treatment, card style, spacing rhythm, button shape, and footer treatment for this domain.
